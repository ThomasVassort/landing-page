Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2023

At Boocle, we place great importance on the protection of our users' personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect the information you provide to us when you use our website and services.

  1. Information We Collect

    We only collect the following information:

    • Name: to personalize communications with you.
    • Email address: to contact you directly, respond to your requests, and send you updates on our products and services.
    • Message: to respond to your inquiry or question.

    We do not collect sensitive information such as health data or financial information.

  2. How We Use Information

    We use the information we collect to:

    • Respond to your inquiries and questions.
    • Send you updates on our products and services, if you have given us your consent to do so.
    • Improve our service.
    • Fulfill our legal and regulatory obligations.

  3. How We Share Information

    We do not share your information with third parties unless it is necessary to provide our service or if we are required to do so by law.

  4. How We Protect Information

    We have implemented security measures to protect the information we collect from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

  5. User Rights

    You have the following rights under applicable data protection laws:

    • Right of access: You can request access to the information we have collected about you.
    • Right to rectification: You can request that any inaccurate information we have collected about you be corrected.
    • Right to erasure: You can request that the information we have collected about you be erased.
    • Right to restriction of processing: You can request that we restrict the processing of your personal information.
    • Right to data portability: You can request that the information we have collected about you be provided to you in a structured and commonly used format.
    • Right to object: You can object to the processing of your personal information for reasons related to your particular situation.

    To exercise your rights, please contact us at the following email address: contact@boocle.io.

  6. Cookies

    We use cookies to improve your browsing experience on our website. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They allow us to recognize your browser and provide you with personalized content.

    You can control the use of cookies by modifying your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect some features of our website.
